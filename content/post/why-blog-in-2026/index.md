Why I decided to create and try to maintain a (mainky tech) blog in 2026? This is a question that I often demand myself and then I decided: why not create a post to talk about it?

So, I work with tech for the last 10 years or so. Something between 4 and 5 years, I wrote a lot of code, being officially defined as software developer or programmer. For the other years, I still did a lot of programming, but I also start to create and mantain some documentation, create learning material for my peers and newcomers. 

While working I wrote a master's dissertation and after some years, I decided to do a PhD on the area and wrote some scientific papers and a thesis.
